Unleash your Insights
Description
Paper presentation enables you to unveil your ideas and gain exposure to various insightful concepts. You can reveal the truths behind unexplored phenomena in the extensive world of science and technology by exhibiting your ingenious thoughts and let your innovations speak through your Paper.
|
Rules
  • ● You may deliver your work on any topic related to Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.
  • ● A team can consist of maximum of 4 members.
  • ● Your abstract must be done in Powerpoint & submitted (online) through google form provided during registration.
  • ● The submissions date is */5/2022, Please make sure to submit before the deadline.
  • ● Your work will be assessed based on Innovation, Comprehension & Presentation.
  • ● Selected team/students in round-1 will move on to the next round held offline.
